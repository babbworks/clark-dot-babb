How to help

Participation should be specific.

Clark is looking for people who can help make the first centre real. That means space, leadership, skill, tools, data, introductions, financing, and accountable support.

clark@babb.tel

Host facility

If you have controlled space, ESD discipline, or a real working floor, Clark can discuss a first centre or training partnership.

Instructor

If you can teach IPC, electronics, repair, test, firmware, or related industrial work, Clark wants to talk.

Local leader

If you can help convene the facility, the school, the municipality, or the industrial network, you may be able to help initiate the centre itself.

Institution

Colleges, workforce groups, municipalities, and industry associations can help bridge the gap between interest and actual operating access.

Investor

Capital is welcome when it follows the evidence. Clark can discuss data access, centre support, and proof-case funding without compromising editorial control.

Acquisition partner

If you represent a machine shop or related facility with aging ownership, succession pressure, or underused capacity, Clark can discuss preservation through partnership, conversion, or transaction where justified.

What to send first

  • What you can offer.
  • Where you are based.
  • What kind of first conversation you want.
  • Any relevant operating details.
  • Any evidence, links, or background material.
  • Anything that would make a next step obvious.
The ask

Bring something concrete.

A room, a bench, a skill, a contact, a succession question, an aging facility, a financing lead, a problem, or a useful correction. Clark is built to work with real input, not vague support.
