2026-06-18

Learning and Working became the primary public frame

The site now centers Clark on two legs: Learning, which includes reporting, connecting, and certification; and Working, which includes production, cooperation, and financing. The Niagara Assembly Centre remains the first hypothesized test case, not a Clark-owned asset.

2026-06-18

Capacity preservation was made explicit

The public argument now says what the network should do when ownership ages out or a shop declines: preserve productive capacity through partnership, conversion, or acquisition where justified.

The Call to Arms moved into the library

The industrial-recovery article now exists as a standalone dispatch and a full library page, so the short public page can point readers to the source form without burying the narrative.

The evidence surface and claims register were tightened

The site now has a clearer split between what is safe to say, what needs validation, and what stays rejected or merely hypothesized. That keeps the site persuasive without letting it drift into story mode.
