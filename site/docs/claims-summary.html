Claim hygiene

The site should never outrun the register.

This summary is the public-facing version of the claim discipline. Use it before repeating a claim outside the site.

Safe to use

  • Clark frames itself around Learning and Working.
  • Learning means reporting, connecting, and certification.
  • Working means production, cooperation, and financing.
  • Clarkware is bundled into centre capability rather than treated as a separate upsell.
  • Niagara Assembly Centre is the first test case, not a Clark-owned facility.
  • Clark recognizes acquisition and conversion as a way to preserve productive capacity when ownership ages out.

Needs validation

  • Corridor superiority over alternatives.
  • Project-specific incentive applicability.
  • Specific centre formation, partner commitments, and transaction structures.
  • Some external operating assumptions that depend on entity and site structure.

Rejected

  • Dunkirk as an active footprint.
  • Workforce housing as current scope.
  • START-UP NY as active planning language.
Rule

If it is not safe to use, it stays labeled.

That is how the site stays persuasive without turning into a story engine.
